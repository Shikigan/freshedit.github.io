Eko Solar Wall Light

ACCESSWAY AND FOOTPATH LIGHTING

The Eko Solar Wall Light is an energy-efficient, motion-activated outdoor lighting solution ideal for illuminating walkways, accessways, and side entrances. Designed with durability and performance in mind, this eco-friendly solar fixture offers intelligent lighting modes, premium materials, and weather resistance to enhance safety and visibility at night without increasing your power bill.

  • Solar powered for off-grid convenience
  • Three smart operating modes to conserve power
  • IP65-rated for all-weather performance
  • Built-in PIR motion sensor for automatic activation
  • Low-copper aluminum body for extended corrosion resistance
  • Powder-coated finish for added durability
  • UV-resistant polycarbonate top cover
